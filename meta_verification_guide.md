## Phase 3: Domain Verification on InterServer
Meta requires you to "Verify" you own the domain.
1. In Meta Business Suite, go to **Business Settings > Brand Safety > Domains**.
2. Add your domain.
3. Choose the **"DNS TXT record"** method.
4. Log into your **InterServer cPanel**.
5. Find **"Zone Editor"**.
6. Select your domain and click **"Manage"**.
7. Click **"Add Record"** and choose type **TXT**.
8. Paste the code from Meta.
9. Wait 10-15 minutes, then click **Verify Domain** in Meta.
